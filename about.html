About Data is your best friend

This blog is created just as a notebook of lessons learned of a Data Engineer trying to make sense of constant-changing crazy Big Data ecosystem.

The path of Data Engineer is hard, fuzzy and still widely undiscovered. The fact that this is an unraveled world within Software Engineering makes the journey way more appealing, There are so many thing we have not invented yet and so many that we have but half of us still don't know how to use.
